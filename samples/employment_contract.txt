# Sample Contract 1
Contract: Employment Agreement
Date: 2024-03-15
Parties: ACME Corporation and John Doe
Type: Employment Contract

Section: Term of Employment
The term of employment shall commence on April 1, 2024, and continue for a period of one year unless terminated earlier in accordance with this Agreement.

Section: Compensation
The Employee shall receive an annual base salary of $75,000, payable in accordance with the Company's standard payroll practices.

Section: Duties
The Employee shall serve as Software Engineer and shall perform all duties and responsibilities typically associated with such position.

Clause: Non-Disclosure
The Employee agrees to maintain the confidentiality of all proprietary information obtained during employment.

Clause: Governing Law
This Agreement shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of the State of California.
